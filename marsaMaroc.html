Management of the valet terminal


This project consists of creating a new management system for the valet terminal containing a web platform and a mobile application, taking into account all the procedures that are part of the great process that MARSA MAROC uses to manage the flow of traffic from the docking of the ship to the delivery of vehicles. Our project includes the analysis of the existing system and given the major problems and anomalies that we found, this new system is realized with the aim of simplifying the navigation and management steps, to meet the needs of the department, to visualize the parking cartography of the valet terminal for the five levels as well as to follow the traffic statistics for imports and exports. using, Nodejs & Express, Ionic with Angular for the mobile app.
