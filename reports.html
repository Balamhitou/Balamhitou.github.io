Reports Scheduler

The project focuses on creating a scheduled reporting system that allows users to plan and schedule reports according to their preferences. The system provides flexibility by allowing users to choose the frequency of reports, such as daily or weekly, and the number of times per week or month. The project uses a combination of technologies such as Cron, NodeJS, TypeORM, PostgreSQL and Elasticsearch to provide a robust and scalable solution. With the scheduled reporting system, users can automate the generation of reports and receive them automatically, saving time and efficiency.
