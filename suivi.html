Project progress monitoring


The project consists in developing a webGIS and mobile solution in order to improve the management and the real time monitoring of the projects of the Agency for the Promotion and Development of the North (APDN). Indeed, this work allows to automate the administrative part by simplifying the work of field workers, which saves a considerable amount of time in generating progress reports and progress reports and site reports. It also makes it easy to structure the processes and the reception of notifications when a project progress is updated. By the application allows to visualize data and analyze them on an interactive 2D map by applying applying spatial queries with geolocation and data filtering tools, as well as the cartographic representation of cartographic representation of statistical data.
