Alert

The project focuses on creating a system of scheduled alerts that allows users to schedule them and program them according to their preferences and unscheduled alerts they trigger with new incoming positon . The system offers flexibility by allowing users to choose the frequency of alerts, for example daily or weekly, and the number of times per week or month. The project uses a combination of technologies such as Cron, NodeJS, TypeScript, TypeORM, PostgreSQL and Elasticsearch, docker provide a robust and scalable solution.
